About Us

Kartavya Karni Seva Foundation, established on January 1st, 2023, by a group of friends, is dedicated to serving humanity with a deep sense of compassion and responsibility. What started as small, heartfelt efforts quickly grew into a larger movement, as more people came together to help those in need. Our initiatives focus on food drives, educational support, healthcare, and animal welfare, with the goal of making a meaningful impact on individuals, families, and communities. The foundation's work is grounded in the belief that serving humanity is our foremost duty. By uplifting the lives of the underprivileged and bringing hope to those in despair, we aim to create a ripple effect of kindness and support. As we continue to grow, we are committed to expanding our reach through health camps, blood donation drives, and educational funding, ensuring that every person in need receives the care and resources they deserve.

What We Do

Food & Essentials Distribution

We provide food, clothes, and other essentials to those in need, including families, children, and elderly individuals in underprivileged communities.

Educational Support

We believe in the power of education. Through book and material distribution, we ensure children from underserved areas have the resources they need to thrive and succeed in their studies.

Healthcare Initiatives

We conduct health camps and provide medical support to marginalized communities, ensuring access to basic healthcare and raising awareness about health issues in remote areas.

Animal Welfare

We support animal shelters, feed street dogs, and provide food for cows at Gaushalas, ensuring that every animal, receives the care and respect they deserve.
