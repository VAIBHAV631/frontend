Who We Are And What We Do...

Kartavya Karni Seva Foundation was founded on 1st January 2023 by a close-knit group of friends who believed in the power of giving. What started as small contributions and heartfelt efforts soon transformed into a larger mission — to serve the underprivileged with compassion, dignity, and hope.
