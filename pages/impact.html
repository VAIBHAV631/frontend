Milestones Achieved

  • Support for Cancer Patients

    Over time, we have had the opportunity to support cancer patients and their families at Tata Memorial Hospital through food drives and blanket distribution. Our volunteers served hot, nutritious meals and warm blankets, offering not just essentials but also emotional comfort during some of their most challenging times. These efforts helped ease their physical and emotional burdens and brought a sense of dignity and care to their journey.

  • Educational Aid for Adivasi Children

    We extended our mission to the Adivasi communities near the National Park, where we provided study materials, books, and educational resources to children who lacked access to quality learning tools. In addition, we created a small, safe play zone to promote joyful and healthy growth. These initiatives fostered both educational and emotional support for children often left out of mainstream development.

  • Ration Distribution for Widows & Differently-Abled

    Our outreach included delivering ration kits to widows and differently-abled individuals across areas from Mira Road to Jogeshwari. Each kit was carefully assembled with food staples and daily essentials, ensuring families had the basic necessities to survive. With compassion and care, our team made home visits to ensure the aid reached the most deserving households, creating a consistent lifeline for many.

  • Animal Welfare & Community Joy

    Beyond human care, we made it our priority to serve animals too. We supported over 600 cows in local Gaushalas with regular fodder donations and fed over 200 street dogs across city corners. Alongside this, we brought smiles to children in orphanages and pediatric wards by donating books, toys, and food. Our visits to old-age homes to share essentials and companionship showed that our vision of service includes every soul in need—be it children, animals, or the elderly.
